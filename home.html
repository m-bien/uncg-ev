WELCOME TO UNCG EV

This UNCG EV website improves the EV charging experience for faculty, students, and staff by addressing inefficiencies in the first-come, first-serve system. It offers real-time notifications on charger availability, including charging reservations and reminders. UNCG EV supports UNCG's sustainability efforts by reducing wait times and optimizing charger use.
